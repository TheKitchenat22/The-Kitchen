Put your restaurant images here:

  logo.png          → header logo (recommended: PNG with transparent background, ~200–400px wide)
  restaurant.jpg    → main hero / cover photo (recommended: wide photo, ~1600×1000 or larger)
  products/         → one photo per menu item (see products/README.txt)

  Product photo example:
    products/d-espresso.jpg
    products/f-burger.jpg

Supported formats: .png, .jpg, .jpeg, .webp

After adding files: commit + push (GitHub Desktop), then hard-refresh the live site.
